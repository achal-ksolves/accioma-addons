Customer Credit Time Limit

Configuration On Customer form


When Confirmed a Sale Order it will check the todays date and due date of previous due Invoice:
  • The due days and compares it with the credit time limit of the customer. If the credit time limit is less it does not allow to confirm the Sale Order.

UserError / Warning

*Allow to over credit time limit? * is Checked then the System will not check for the credit limits.
